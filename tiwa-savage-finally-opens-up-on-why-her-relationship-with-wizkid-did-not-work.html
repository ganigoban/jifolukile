Tiwa Savage Finally Opens Up On Why Her Relationship With Wizkid Did Not Work

Posted by Tandra Barner on Wednesday, August 21, 2024

Wondering why top Nigerian Afrobeat queen Tiwa Savage’s relationship with the Starboy Music Label CEO, Wizkid did not work out?

For those who may not know, Tiwa Savage has a spell with Wizkid, which lasted for over a year and if you are a fan of any of them you would recall that the two made a public display of their affection for each other in music videos and on the music stage during performances.

After divorcing the father of his son, Teebillz and falling out of her relationship with Wizkid, she has not been seen with a man who has been declared by her officially that she is now dating.

In her latest post on Instagram, Tiwa Savage subtly explained why till now she is still single although she is a meal most men would want to have on their dining table always.

The former Mavins Records signee revealed that she does not want to be any man’s second choice because she is the first and only daughter of her father.

Her disclosure implied that although she would fall in love with a man in a relationship if will not want to become an option or better yet have to be dating a man who has several partners.
