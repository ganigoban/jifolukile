See Kylie Jenner Look Blissful in a Breezy Embroidered Top and Jeans

Posted by Tandra Barner on Monday, August 26, 2024

Kylie Jenner just stepped out in her most wearable outfit yet.

The Kylie Cosmetics mogul surprised fans with her latest Instagram post, which includes a series of sunlit photos from a lovely outdoor meal and stroll through the garden. In the images, the stylish reality star—who's best known for edgy, skintight, boundary-pushing ensembles—is dressed in a lovely casual look, composed of baggy blue Levi jeans and a white sleeveless peasant top with a lacy neckline.

She added classic black sandals, a small gold watch, and layered bracelets, while carrying a large straw tote from BTB Los Angeles. Her natural makeup look revealed her natural freckles, and she channeled a romantic vibe with peach blush and a rosy nude lip.

Kylie Jenner//Instagram

Kylie Jenner//Instagram

While the low-key outfit may be the biggest departure from the reality star's usual sartorial preferences, Jenner has embraced a more delicate, feminine style over the past year. Last July, the star wore in a frilly white two-piece set composed of a buttoned bandeau top and a breezy pleated skirt. And in June, she shared glowing pics of herself dressed in a dreamy cottagecore dress by Dôen which featured a smocked bodice, puffed off-the-shoulder sleeves, and a delicate blue floral pattern.

The first hints of the star's style reinvention came during her fashion-filled trip to Paris for Fashion Week last May, where she stepped out in several quiet luxury looks including an elegant white Alaïa midi dress and a black halter dress with a flowing skirt by Bottega Veneta. She completed both looks with minimal accessories and sleek black sunglasses.
