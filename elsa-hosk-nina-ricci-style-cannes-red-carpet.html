Elsa Hosk Wears Wings on an Angelic Feathered Gown in Cannes

Posted by Kelle Repass on Saturday, August 24, 2024

Elsa Hosk stepped onto a prestigious red carpet on the French Riviera this weekend, and it wasn’t for the 77th Cannes Film Festival, which comes to a close today.

Instead, the Swedish model was there to attend the amfAR Gala Cannes, a revered annual charity event that raises funds and awareness for HIV and AIDS research. This year’s gala was hosted by Demi Moore, sponsored by Chopard, and included a dinner, runway show, and auction, as well as performances by Nick Jonas and Jess Glynne, and even a surprise musical moment courtesy of Cher.

Daniele Venturelli

Hosk fit into the event’s glitz and glamour with a feathered white gown and black opera gloves. Her dress, pulled from Nina Ricci’s Spring/Summer ’24 collection, featured a body-hugging fit with a puffy, capelike neckline decorated with dangling black feathers. The neckline’s exaggerated silhouette gave the appearance of wings—marking another pair for the longtime Victoria’s Secret model. Black satin gloves switched up the look from its original runway styling, which used leather gloves.

Hosk accessorized regally with a sparkling micro evening bag from Saint Laurent, as well as black diamond clip-on earrings from Tiffany & Co. Shimmering black open-toed Rendez-Vous sandals, also from Saint Laurent, peeked out from a slit at the back of her dress.

To match the elegant look of her gown, Hosk swept her blonde hair into a romantic updo, leaving out a few curls to frame her face. She kept her glam minimal, emphasizing her blue eyes with fluttery false lashes and finishing off her look with a brown lip liner and pink lipstick.
