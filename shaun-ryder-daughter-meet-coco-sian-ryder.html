Shaun Ryder Daughter: Meet Coco Sian Ryder

Posted by Patria Henriques on Saturday, August 10, 2024

Shaun William George Ryder is an English singer, songwriter, and poet. As lead singer of Happy Mondays, he was a leading figure in the Madchester cultural scene during the late 1980s and early 1990s.

In 1993, he formed Black Grape with former Happy Mondays dancer.

Meet Coco Sian Ryder

Coco was born in Manchester as a result of her mother’s connection to Shaun Ryder.
Coco is one of Ryder’s six children by four different women.

It is well known that Ryder had an addiction in the past; he discussed it in his memoirs Twisting My Melon. Coco was reared by her mother alone.

Coco claims that she is not in contact with her father.
