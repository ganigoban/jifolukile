WWE Fans Chanted LA Knights Catchphrase At MJF During AEW Dynamite

Posted by Kelle Repass on Sunday, September 8, 2024

AEW brought their show to Columbus, Ohio this week, but those fans in the Buckeye State might have rather seen LA Knight. During AEW Dynamite this week, MJF’s promo received a dose of WWE from fan chants.

WWE’s standout star, LA Knight, continues to gather more support with each week, and that’s not changing. It appears that there were quite a few LA Knight fans in the crowd during AEW Dynamite this week.

LA Knight has captured the attention of the WWE Universe, who now enthusiastically echo his “Yeah” chant during his promos, just like Stone Cold’s “What?” chants. While this became a regular feature of Knight’s promos, it appears that followers of AEW are also adopting this trend.

One fan with an LA Knight X account called @YEAHMovement_, took to social media to highlight that the “Yeah” chants made an appearance even during MJF’s Dynamite promo.

This is incredible. The crowd on #AEWDynamite said “YEAH” during @The_MJF’s promo tonight. The #YEAHMovement truly is taking over the world of professional wrestling.

In a noteworthy development, MJF and Adam Cole are slated to clash for the AEW World Championship at the All In event. Fans will need to tune into the pay-per-view to see how everything goes down.

LA Knight’s popularity is also causing fans to go through his old footage. Video of Paul Bearer’s final performance before his passing has resurfaced recently, and LA Knight was right alongside the WWE Hall of Famer. Odds are, LA Knight will create many more memories to come down the line.

What’s your take on this situation with LA Knight’s YEAH? Will fans soon take it and make it something bigger than just WWE? Sound off in the comments to let us know what you think!
