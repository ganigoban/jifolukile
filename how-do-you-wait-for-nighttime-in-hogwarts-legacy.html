How do you wait for nighttime in Hogwarts Legacy?

Posted by Billy Koelling on Saturday, August 31, 2024

To wait for nighttime in Hogwarts Legacy, follow these steps:
1. Open up your Map screen.
2. Press R3 to Wait.
3. If it’s daytime, your game will change to night.

Now, let’s answer some related questions:

Can you speed up night time in Hogwarts Legacy?

In order to change the time of day in Hogwarts Legacy, you can press R3 to Wait and your game will change to night if it is currently daytime. However, there is no way to advance hour-by-hour.

Can you sleep or wait in Hogwarts Legacy?

Although Hogwarts Legacy features a dormitory and a bed, you cannot sleep in the game. Instead, you can pass time by waiting, which will skip from day to night or night to day.

Can you only collect Demiguise at night?

Yes, you can only collect Demiguise statues at nighttime in Hogwarts Legacy. It is important to refrain from looking for them during the day.

Why can’t I pick up Demiguise?

In Hogwarts Legacy, you can only collect Demiguise Moons during the night. If you come across one during the wrong hour, you will need to use the map and wait until nighttime.

Can you walk around at night in Hogwarts Legacy?

Yes, in Hogwarts Legacy, you are free to roam the castle anytime at night. It is not restricted, and there is no curfew enforced in the game.

Is there a curfew in Hogwarts Legacy?

No, there is no curfew in Hogwarts Legacy. The game does not enforce any curfew rules or regulations that were present in the books.

Does it rain in Hogwarts Legacy?

Yes, it does rain in Hogwarts Legacy. The game includes detailed rain effects, and even small raindrops can be seen hitting your character’s robes.

How do you get Mooncalf in Hogwarts Legacy?

To get Mooncalf in Hogwarts Legacy, you can find them in the Forbidden Forest next to the North Ford Bog Entrance Floo Flame. You’ll need to head south from there and locate the den. It is important to note that Mooncalves can only be found during the night, so if you arrive at a den during the day, you can open your map and click on the “Wait” button to advance time.

Do seasons change in Hogwarts Legacy?

No, seasons do not change in Hogwarts Legacy. Once you complete the main quest line, the seasons will no longer change in the game.

How many seasons of Hogwarts Legacy are there?

Hogwarts Legacy features four seasons: summer, fall, winter, and spring. These seasons are all present and distinct from one another. They showcase your character’s progression and act as a form of fan service.

How many Demiguise statues for level 2?

To progress to Alohomora level 2, you must collect all nine Demiguise Statues on the list and then initiate a conversation with Gladwin Moon.

How do I collect a Demiguise moon?

To collect a Demiguise moon in Hogwarts Legacy, you can only do so when it is nighttime. If you want to find these objects, you will need to periodically advance the day-night cycle via the world map.

Should I choose Sebastian or Natty?

The choice between Sebastian and Natty does not have a significant impact on the game. It does not make much of a difference which one you choose to go with you to Hogsmeade.

Which house is best in Hogwarts Legacy?

There is no definitive answer to which house is the best in Hogwarts Legacy. Each house has its own unique qualities and strengths. However, Hufflepuff is known for being undyingly loyal, hardworking, and fair. They also have access to exclusive side quests.

Does Hogwarts Legacy have multiple endings?

Hogwarts Legacy offers three different endings, regardless of the decisions you make throughout the game. There is no in-game morality system, so every student will have access to all three endings.

Do you get in trouble for being out at night in Hogwarts Legacy?

No, in Hogwarts Legacy, there is no penalty for being out at night. The game does not impose any restrictions or punishments for exploring the castle during nighttime.

Can you be on the dark side in Hogwarts Legacy?

No, in Hogwarts Legacy, you cannot canonically be evil or be recognized as inherently evil. There is no version of the story where you will rise up as a proto-Voldemort.

How many hours can you put into Hogwarts Legacy?

The total gameplay time in Hogwarts Legacy can vary depending on your playstyle and how much of the exploration and additional content you engage with. If you focus mainly on the main quests, you can expect to beat the game in around 20-25 hours.
