Who are Dariq Whiteheads parents?

Born on August 1, 2004, in New Jersey, United States, Dariq Whitehead is an American college basketball player for the Duke Blue Devils of the Atlantic Coast Conference (ACC).

He is well-known for his fast jump shot from any area inside the arc and has a very quick first step.

Dariq Whitehead’s parents?
