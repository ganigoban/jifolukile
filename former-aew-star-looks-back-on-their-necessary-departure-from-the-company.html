Former AEW Star Looks Back on Their Necessary Departure from the Company

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, September 2, 2024

Earlier this year, Frankie Kazarian, a former AEW star and one-half of the inaugural AEW World Tag Team Champions alongside Scorpio Sky in SCU, made the decision to part ways with AEW and return to IMPACT Wrestling, where he initially found success in his wrestling career. Kazarian has now taken to Twitter to reflect on his choice, describing it as both difficult and necessary while encouraging others to take similar steps in their careers.

In his tweet, Kazarian stated:

“In January I made a difficult, but VERY necessary decision. I decided to bet on myself. It resulted in the most rewarding, successful & overall fun years of my career. I urge all of you who may be in a similar situation that action speaks louder than words. Don’t talk it, walk it.”

Kazarian’s departure from AEW marked a significant move in his career, and it’s clear from his words that he views it as a positive and transformative experience. His message serves as a reminder that sometimes taking bold steps and believing in oneself can lead to remarkable personal and professional growth.
