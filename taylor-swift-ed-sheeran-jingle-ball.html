Taylor Swift Makes Glorious Return to the Stage With BFF Ed Sheeran

Posted by Kelle Repass on Wednesday, August 28, 2024

Christmas came early for the lucky attendees of KIIS-FM's Jingle Ball in L.A. last night.

Fans were undeniably excited to see Taylor Swift back on stage after her hiatus from performing, and as if her return wasn't enough, the 27-year-old singer thrilled the audience with a joint-performance with close friend Ed Sheeran.

This year's Jingle Ball featured an impressive lineup of artists, including Demi Lovato, The Chainsmokers, Halsey, Liam Payne, Niall Horan, and more, and we're kind of jealous of everyone who got to attend. In addition to all these amazing acts, concert-goers got to witness the epic return of T.Swift, who hasn't performed at a big venue since the Super Bowl in February.

Swift, who recently released her new album Reputation, took the stage in a gold snakeskin top and black short shorts, which she paired with black-and-gold combat boots. She opened her set with "...Ready For It?" before performing several older hits, including "Blank Space" and "Shake It Off."

However, "End Game" was arguably the highlight of her performance, as singer Ed Sheeran joined Swift for his verse on the track, sending fans into a tizzy.
