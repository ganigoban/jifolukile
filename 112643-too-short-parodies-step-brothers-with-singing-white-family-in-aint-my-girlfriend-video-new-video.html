Too Short Parodies &quot;Step Brothers&quot; With White Family In &quot;Ain't My Girlfriend&quot; Video

Posted by Billy Koelling on Thursday, August 22, 2024

Too $hort released "Ain't My Girlfriend" almost two years ago. The single featured Ty Dolla $ign, Jeremih, and French Montana, and was supposed to appear on his The Pimp Tape project. After several delays, Too Short is gearing up to bring the project to the people (hopefully) and his latest music video is a push in the right direction. Too $hort gives Joyner Lucas a verse on the updated single, and the visuals are a direct nod to the comedy Step Brothers. 

In the Will Ferrell and John C. Reilly flick, the "perfect" family, led by Adam Scott, take a car ride where they sing in harmony. Director Embryo opted to use that scene as the inspiration for the "Ain't My Girlfriend" video, and the artists on the song are not even featured in the visuals. Too $hort does make a quick cameo, but other than that, the full attention is focused on the performing white family. The concept is dope, but it won't be long before someone lashes out at Too $hort for releasing a video where a white family lip syncs the N-Word.
