Yevgeny Prigozhin Net Worth At The Time of Death

Posted by Tandra Barner on Tuesday, August 13, 2024

Up until June 2023, Yevgeny Prigozhin was a well-known Russian tycoon, mercenary commander, and close associate of Russian President Vladimir Putin.

Prigozhin frequently goes by the moniker “Putin’s chef” since he owns eateries and catering businesses that work for the Kremlin.

A private aircraft travelling from Moscow to St. Petersburg on Wednesday, August 23, 2023, crashed in Russia’s Tver Region.

All 10 on board perished, according to the Russian Emergencies Ministry.

In a later announcement from Rosaviation, Yevgeny Prigozhin, the director of the Wagner Private Military Company, was named as a passenger.

In the northwest Tver Region, the accident took place close to the community of Kuzhenkino.

Yevgeny Prigozhin Income Source

Yevgeny Prigozhin’s primary source of income is believed to be his involvement in various businesses, particularly in the field of catering and food services.

He is often referred to as “Putin’s chef” because his companies have reportedly provided catering services to the Russian government and the Kremlin.

Prigozhin is also associated with the Internet Research Agency (IRA), a Russian organization accused of engaging in online influence operations, including efforts to interfere in foreign elections.

However, the exact financial details of his involvement with the IRA are not publicly disclosed.

Additionally, Prigozhin has been linked to private military companies, most notably the Wagner Group, which has reportedly been involved in conflicts in Ukraine, Syria, and other regions.

These private military ventures could potentially be another source of income for him, although the financial aspects of these activities are often shrouded in secrecy.

It’s important to note that Prigozhin’s financial dealings are not well-documented, and much of his wealth and income sources remain opaque and subject to speculation and controversy.
